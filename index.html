Before the age of Jordan

In the prehistoric era of basketball, it is generally believed that small people are more suitable for playing basketball than big people. But at that time, the appearance of big men represented by George McCann strongly attacked people's doubts. On June 6, 1946, baa, the predecessor of NBA, was founded in the United States. All this originated from the fact that the owners of 11 ice hockey stadiums and gymnasiums did not want to idle their stadiums when there were no related games. For this purpose, the American Basketball League came into being

The age of Jordan

The 1990s is a time when NBA is going global. With the rise of Jordan and bull, NBA's communication power and influence are further spread. Jordan's influence benefits NBA and its personal brand sponsors. Jordan effect has spread since the late 1980s. After Jordan entered the bulls, the attendance rate of the team's home court began to rise. In 1988, Jordan's MVP season, the Bulls' home court was almost full

New era

In the 1996 NBA draft, Kobe was selected by the Charlotte Hornets in the 13th place in the first round, and then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
